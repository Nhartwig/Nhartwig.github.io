Analogue PID Controlled MagLev Coil

This was a fun project I got to work on while in the lab at Philips Color Kinetics. Usually, magnetic levitators have their PID control system implemented through a microcontroller like Arduino. However, a friend of mine at work suggested I do it in pure hardware for a greater challenge! After many hours on the scope, and some soldering iron burns (did you really do hardware if you didn't get soldering iron burns?), I had this system hacked together.

  • PID control
  • Power Design
